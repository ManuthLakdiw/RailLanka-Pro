About Us

Our Story

Rail Lanka Pro was established in 2005 with a vision to revolutionize railway travel in Sri Lanka. We started with just two routes and have now expanded to cover the entire island, connecting major cities and scenic destinations.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and reliable railway transportation while showcasing the beautiful landscapes of Sri Lanka. We are committed to sustainable travel and reducing our carbon footprint.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced railway professionals, hospitality experts, and technology innovators working together to deliver an exceptional travel experience for our passengers.

Our Achievements

- Awarded "Best Railway Service" in Sri Lanka for 5 consecutive years
- Carried over 5 million passengers safely
- Introduced eco-friendly trains reducing carbon emissions by 30%
- First railway company in Sri Lanka to implement online booking
